02 / REMEDIATION AND PREVENTION ACTION ITEMS
Remediation Key Takeaways
1. Identity Separation is Non-Negotiable
The shared default service account (CCR-002) enabled cross-namespace attacks. Isolating permissions is the top priority.
2. Automation Parity is required
Manual troubleshooting cannot compete with API-speed loops. Defense needs proactive configuration sweep scripts.
3. Preventive Declarative Controls
Deploy Pod Disruption Budgets (PDBs) and Horizontal Pod Autoscalers (HPAs) rather than relying on reactive scripts.
